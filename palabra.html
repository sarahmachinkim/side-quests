PALABRA

A crossword app that teaches languages that Latin-based languages have in common.

Preview

Problem

People often feel insecure and hesitant when learning a new language. The fear of making mistakes and the overwhelming number of unfamiliar words can make the process intimidating.

Solution

Many languages, particularly those of Latin origin (Spanish, French, Portuguese, Italian), share a vast number of common words. By leveraging these similarities, we can make language learning feel more intuitive and less daunting.

Execution

Palabra is a React Native gamified language-learning experience that tells the story of linguistic commonalities in an engaging and memorable way. Through interactive crosswords and vivid illustrations, users naturally absorb new vocabulary by recognizing patterns rather than rote memorization.

Why This Works

Cognitive science suggests that learning is more effective when new information is connected to existing knowledge. Instead of treating a new language as something entirely foreign, Palabra helps users anchor new words to familiar ones, making retention easier and boosting confidence.
