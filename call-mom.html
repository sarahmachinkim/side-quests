CALL MOM

A Chrome extension to check if you can call relatives in other time zones.

Problem

People with relatives abroad know how frustrating it can be. You want to call your mom, so you google "time in Korea" only to discover it’s the middle of the night – and before you know it, several days have gone by.

Solution

A Google Chrome extension where you enter the time your mom goes to bed and wakes up, along with her location. The plug-in then shows you whether you should call her, or, if not, tells you how long until you can.

Execution

A very simple HTML extension that combines data input fields with time zones in a user-friendly design reminiscent of fridge magnets.

Why This Works

It's simple, yet it still allows a degree of spontaneity when staying in touch – removing the extra steps of remembering to call, checking time zones, doing the calculations, and confirming that it’s an appropriate hour to ring.
